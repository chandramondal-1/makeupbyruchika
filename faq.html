Common Questions

Everything you need to know before your big day

How early should I book for my wedding?
I recommend booking at least 4-6 months in advance, especially for wedding seasons (Winter & Summer), to ensure availability for your special date.
Do you travel to the client's location?
Yes, I provide door-step services for bridal and group bookings worldwide. Travel and logistics fees apply based on distance from Durgapur.
What brands do you use?
I use only premium, high-end brands like MAC, Huda Beauty, Dior, Estee Lauder, and Anastasia Beverly Hills to ensure the best quality and longevity for your makeup.
Is a trial session available?
Yes! I highly recommend a trial session to discuss skin types, products, and to finalize your dream look. Trials are typically held at my Durgapur studio.
Is advance payment needed?
Yes, a 50% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. The remaining balance is due on the day of the event.
Do you provide hair styling and saree draping?
Yes, most of my bridal packages include basic hair styling and saree/lehenga draping. Advanced hairstyles may involve additional charges.
